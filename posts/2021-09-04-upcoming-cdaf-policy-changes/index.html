Upcoming CDAF Policy Changes

September 4, 2021

Vagrant Rationalisation, Terraform Adoption

While support for Vagrant will continue, the extend of regression testing for it will be reduced, i.e. only use cases that I cannot replace with docker will continue to be regression tested. Additional efforts will be make for “Manifest Releases” in Terraform (based on Autonomous Development, Authoritative Release. & Containers at Scale, A Containers Journey.

CentOS Sunset, UBI8

With the end of CentOS in the infancy of CentOS 8, Fedora support by CDAF will based on Red Hat Universal Base Image (UBI). Debian support will continue to be based on Ubuntu.

Note: CentOS 7 will continue to be supported until it’s end-of-life, 30 Jun 2024.
